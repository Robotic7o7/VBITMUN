About VBIT MUN 1.0.

The situation that the world is currently in creates, a dire need for leaders, diplomats and thinkers who are the epitome of wisdom and intellect. VBIT aims to inspire the youth to follow tenets of wisdom in all aspects of their lives through it’s first ever MUN, VBIT MUN 1.0. It holds the power and potential to unite the young minds.

Model United Nations is an educational simulation in which, students act as delegates representing various countries in an attempt to resolve complex global problems by diplomacy and critical thinking. The entire model aims to provide delegates with a simulated political experience and allows for the refinement of skills in teamwork, debating, research and analysis. VBIT MUN 1.0 comprises of various committees, delicately crafted to incite critical thinking, break ideological barriers and inculcate accountability. An enthralling experience, Model United Nation’s empower students with a unique perspective and a perspicuous understanding of the idiosyncrasies of impending world affairs.
